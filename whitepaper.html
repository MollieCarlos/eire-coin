EIREBSC Whitepaper

Executive Summary

EIREBSC is a BEP-20 token on the Binance Smart Chain (BSC) engineered to promote long-term engagement and ecosystem sustainability. With a fixed total supply of 1,000,000,000 tokens, EIREBSC features a 3% buy tax sourced from a pre-funded vault, distributing rewards to treasury, liquidity, and staking reflections. This design balances buyer fairness, automatic funding, and holder incentives, minimizing short-term speculation while fostering growth. View the contract on BscScan for full transparency: EIREBSC Contract. As a developing project, ownership remains active to facilitate refinements, with renouncement planned post-stabilization. Treasury taxes will fund investments, with profits reinvested to enhance token value.
