Buy Transactions: Step-by-Step Breakdown

Buys are the lifeblood of EIREBSC's growth engine, transforming each purchase into a catalyst for ecosystem enhancement. Here's what happens in depth when a user buys tokens on PancakeSwap (or any compatible DEX):

  1. Detection and Validation: The contract's `_transfer` function identifies the transaction as a buy by checking if the sender is the PancakeSwap pair address (set via `setPancakePair`) and the recipient is a valid user wallet. If the buyer is new (no prior `firstHoldTime`), the current block timestamp is recorded as their hold start date. This timestamp is crucial for future sell penalties and vesting calculations.
  2. Tax Calculation: A flat 3% tax is computed on the full transfer amount (e.g., for a 10,000 token buy, tax = 300 tokens). Importantly, this tax is not deducted from the buyer's amount—the buyer receives 100% of their purchased tokens. Instead, the tax is sourced from the pre-funded vault, ensuring seamless execution without impacting user experience.
  3. Vault Extraction: The main contract calls `extractForTax` on the vault (EIREBSC_Vault), which verifies the caller is the main contract and transfers the exact tax amount back to EIREBSC. This vault-funded model is a key innovation: It pre-allocates tokens (funded by the owner or community) to cover taxes, preventing supply inflation or buyer penalties. If the vault lacks funds, the buy reverts—encouraging proactive funding for sustainability.
  4. Tax Distribution: The extracted tax is divided equally into three portions (1% each of the original amount):
    • 1% to Treasury (EIREBSC_Treasury): Transferred to the treasury contract, which invokes `receiveTax` to split the amount 50/50 between a dividend pool (for potential holder payouts) and a locked pool (for long-term reserves). This portion funds strategic investments—such as partnerships, marketing, or yield-generating opportunities—with all profits reinvested into EIREBSC to buy back tokens, burn supply, or enhance liquidity, directly boosting holder value.
    • 1% to Liquidity Manager (EIREBSC_LiquidityManager): Sent to this contract, which holds predefined swap paths (token to WBNB and vice versa) for future automated liquidity additions or swaps via the PancakeSwap router. This strengthens the trading pool, reducing slippage and improving market depth over time.
    • 1% to Staking Reflections (EIREBSC_StakingReflections): Allocated to the buyer via `vestForUser`, adding to their vested balance with a 365-day vesting period starting from their first hold. This creates passive income, as reflections accumulate from every buy across the network.
  5. Final Transfer and Event Emission: The full original amount is transferred to the buyer. Events are emitted for transparency (e.g., Transfer, TaxExtracted), logged on BscScan for auditing.

This process ensures every buy not only acquires tokens but actively contributes to the token's health—funding investments, bolstering liquidity, and rewarding the community—all without cost to the participant. Over time, as volume increases, this creates a virtuous cycle: more buys lead to deeper liquidity and richer reflections, attracting more holders and driving sustainable appreciation.

Sell Transactions

Sells are streamlined to maintain liquidity while protecting the ecosystem:

  • No additional tax is applied.
  • The contract evaluates hold duration:
  • Early Sell (Within 365 Days): The transaction completes, but any unvested reflections are forfeited via `forfeit` in the staking contract, emitting a Forfeited event. This penalty discourages flippers without blocking exits.
  • Mature Sell (After 365 Days): Unrestricted sale with retention of all vested rewards.

This anti-dump feature stabilizes price by reducing sell pressure, benefiting long-term holders through preserved value and continued reward accumulation.
