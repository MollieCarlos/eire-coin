Empower Your Child's Future with Éire Child Savings

As the world's first fully on-chain crypto child savings account, Éire revolutionizes generational wealth. Parents manage secure, tax-advantaged accounts that grow with your child, auto-rebalancing from 90% stablecoins at birth to 55% at 18 for balanced risk.

How Child Savings Works

Parents add children, deposit up to $5,000/year, get 5% tax credit. Assets grow tax-free, rebalance automatically. At 18, child claims with deferred taxes on gains.

Key Features

  • Parent-managed until 18.
  • Age-based glide path: 90% stables at birth, 55% at maturity.
  • Tax-deferred growth, no taxes on rebalancing.
  • 5% tax credit on deposits from treasury.
  • 10% penalty on early withdrawals.
  • Yield from JustLend (8% APY) on stables.
