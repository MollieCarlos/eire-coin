EIRE Token

EIRE Coin

Introduction

EIRE is a utility token on the Polygon network, designed to link cryptocurrency with Ireland's economy. Transaction taxes fund investments in the Irish Stock Exchange (ISEQ) and affordable housing projects, creating sustainable value for holders.

Tokenomics

  • Total Supply: 1,000,000,000 EIRE (1B initial, reducing to 365M through quarterly burns starting Q3 2026).
  • Tax on Transfers: 3% (2% to treasury for ISEQ/housing, 1% to liquidity, with 1% returnable after 365 days if holding).
  • Burn Mechanism: Quarterly burns based on treasury profits in POL, sent to dead address until 365M supply.
  • Decimals: 8 normally, switching to 2 near final supply.

Key Features

  • Staking & Dividends: Stake EIRE for 1+ year to earn POL dividends from contract balance.
  • Reflections: 1% tax held for 365 days; claimable if holding, or re-minted if sold.
  • Treasury Management: Funds ISEQ investments and housing.
  • Security: Pausable, reentrancy-protected, with manual burn after investment lodgment.

Roadmap

  • Q3 2026: Launch, DEX listing (QuickSwap), community building.
  • Q1 2027: ISEQ integration, staking launch.
  • Q2 2027: DAO governance, housing partnerships, first burns.
  • Q3 2027+: Expand RWAs, CEX listings, continue burns to 365M supply.
