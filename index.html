Full Transparency on Taxes and Metrics

We believe in complete openness. All our tokens feature a 3% buy tax that goes to the treasury for ecosystem development and a 3% sell tax that is burned (with 50% auto-minted back to maintain stability). This ensures deflationary pressure while protecting supply floors at 50 million per token.
