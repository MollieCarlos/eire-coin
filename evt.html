ÉireVault (EVT): The Cornerstone of Your Wealth Strategy

EVT is designed for security and long-term holding, providing a safe haven in volatile markets. With built-in deflationary mechanics and cross-ownership, EVT ensures stability while rewarding holders with 1% APY for extended holding periods.

How EVT Works

EVT leverages Tron's low-cost network for efficient transactions. Deposits are auto-allocated to stablecoins for protection, with rebalancing to maintain optimal risk. Taxes are transparent: 3% buy to treasury, 3% sell burned (50% minted back).

Key Features
